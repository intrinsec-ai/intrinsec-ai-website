Thought-Level Security Signals
Monitor thoughts, not just events.
Traditional security sees what happened at the system boundary. AI agents require visibility into intent, reasoning, and tool-use behaviorwhere attacks take shape first.
Traditional Event-Level
Sees what happened
Monitors system events after they occurlogins, file access, network connections. By the time you see the event, the attack has already progressed.
  • Reactive: detects threats after execution
  • Limited visibility into agent reasoning
  • Cannot see intent before it becomes action
Intrinsec AI Thought-Level
Extracts critical thoughts as signals
Identifies and scores security-relevant conceptsmanipulation, exfiltration planning, privilege-seekingfrom prompts, reasoning, and tool calls. These thought-level signals appear before events.
  • Proactive: detects intent before execution
  • Extracts abstract concepts as security signals
  • No fixed taxonomydiscovers emergent threats
Result: your SOC gets thought-level security signalsextracted critical reasonings and intents that can be monitored, scored, and correlated in SIEM workflows, giving you visibility where attacks take shape first.
